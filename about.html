About Us

Freshly roasted in Singapore. Ethically sourced. Always intentional.

We’re a small-batch coffee roastery based in Singapore, focused on thoughtful sourcing and precise roasting. Each origin is selected for its unique character, then roasted in-house to bring out its best — nothing more, nothing less. No noise, no shortcuts. Just honest coffee, crafted for those who care about the details.
